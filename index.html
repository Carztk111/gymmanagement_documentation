Frequently Asked Questions

For Members

01 How do I register as a member?

Visit the gym or use the GNEX 360 app to create an account. An administrator will verify your details and activate your membership.

02 Can I change my membership plan?

Yes, you can request a plan change through your account. An administrator will review and approve the change.

03 How does QR check-in work?

Simply scan the gym's QR code at check-in using the GNEX 360 app or a standard QR scanner. Your attendance will be automatically recorded.

04 Where can I view my progress?

Your attendance history, progress tracking, and analytics are available in your member dashboard.

For Administrators

01 How do I approve member registrations?

Navigate to the Approvals section in your admin dashboard. Review pending registrations and click Approve or Reject.

02 Can I generate attendance reports?

Yes, the Reports module provides customizable attendance reports, member analytics, and billing summaries.

03 How do I manage coaches?

Use the Employees module to add, edit, and manage coaches. Assign them to classes and set their permissions.

04 Is member data secure?

Yes, GNEX 360 uses role-based access control, encryption, and follows security best practices to protect member data.
