Overview

GNEX 360 provides members with a centralized platform for managing memberships, attendance, QR access, payments, and account information. Members can monitor their gym activity and membership status in real time.

2 Present the QR code at reception.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I edit my profile?

Yes. Members can update profile information.

What happens when my membership expires?

QR access and gym access may be restricted until renewal.

Can I see my attendance history?

Yes. Attendance records are available within your account.
