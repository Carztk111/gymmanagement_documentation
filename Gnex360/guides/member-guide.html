MEMBER GUIDE

Overview

GNEX 360 provides members with a centralized platform for membership management, attendance monitoring, class reservations, QR attendance, subscription renewals, and account maintenance. Members can manage their complete fitness journey through a single integrated system.

My Profile

MEMBER JOURNEY

Member Workflow

The following workflow illustrates the complete member journey from registration to maintaining an active membership in GNEX 360.

Step 1: Registration

Members begin by completing the registration form and submitting the required information.

Step 2: Approval

Management reviews the application and validates membership requirements.

Step 3: Login

Approved members gain access to GNEX 360 using their assigned account credentials.

Step 4: Membership

Members select a subscription package and complete the payment process.

Step 5: Attendance

QR codes are used to record gym attendance and validate membership status.

Step 6: Renewal

Members may renew subscriptions before their membership expires.

REGISTRATION PROCESS

Member Registration

New users must complete the registration process before gaining access to GNEX 360 services and memberships.

Join Now

Click the Join Now button from the landing page to start the membership application process.

Personal Information

Provide your personal details including full name, contact information, and address.

Select Membership

Choose the membership plan that best fits your fitness goals and duration preferences.

Payment Method

Select PayMongo or over-the-counter payment based on your preferred payment option.

Submit Application

Review your information and submit the registration form for approval.

Approval

Wait for management approval before receiving account activation instructions.

ACCOUNT ACTIVATION

Account Activation

After management approves a membership application, the member receives activation instructions and gains access to GNEX 360.

Receive Invitation Email

Approved members receive an invitation email containing activation instructions and account access information.

Access Activation Link

Members open the activation link provided within the invitation email.

Create Credentials

Set up account credentials including password and security information.

Account Activated

The account becomes active and the member may now access all available features.

CLASS RESERVATIONS

Classes & Bookings

Members may browse available classes, reserve slots, monitor booking status, and manage upcoming sessions.

Browse Classes

View available classes together with schedules, instructors, and capacity information.

Submit Reservation

Select a class schedule and submit a booking request.

Pending Approval

Reservation requests remain pending until reviewed by management.

Approved Booking

Approved reservations appear in the Upcoming Classes section.

ATTENDANCE MANAGEMENT

QR Check-In

Members use QR codes to record attendance and verify active membership status.

Generate QR Code

Open the Attendance page to display your personal QR code.

Scan QR Code

Reception personnel scan the member's QR code during gym entry.

Attendance Validation

Membership status is automatically verified during check-in.

Attendance Recording

Attendance records are automatically stored in the system.

ATTENDANCE RECORDS

Attendance History

Members may review previous gym visits and attendance records through the system.

Date

Displays the attendance date.

Time In

Displays the recorded check-in time.

Time Out

Displays the recorded check-out time.

Status

Indicates whether attendance was successfully validated.

MEMBERSHIP MANAGEMENT

Membership Renewal

Members may renew their subscriptions before expiration to maintain uninterrupted access to gym facilities, attendance services, and class reservations.

Open My Profile

Navigate to the My Profile page where membership information is displayed.

Select Renew Membership

Click the Renew Membership button to begin the renewal process.

Choose a Plan

Select the membership package that best matches your requirements.

Submit Payment

Complete payment through the available payment methods supported by the system.

Await Approval

Management reviews and validates the submitted renewal request.

Membership Updated

Once approved, the membership status, duration, and expiration date are updated.

ACCOUNT MANAGEMENT

Account Settings

Members can manage personal information, account credentials, and emergency contacts through the Account Settings page.

Profile Information

Update personal details such as contact information and address.

Password & Security

Change passwords and maintain account security settings.

Emergency Contacts

Add or modify emergency contact information for safety purposes.

Notifications

Manage notification preferences and account alerts.
