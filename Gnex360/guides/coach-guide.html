COACH GUIDE

Overview

Coaches use GNEX 360 to oversee assigned classes, monitor attendance, review member participation, and track fitness progress. The system provides coaches with tools to manage training activities and support members throughout their fitness journey.

COACH PERMISSIONS

Coach Capabilities

  • View assigned schedules
  • Review class participants
  • Monitor attendance records
  • Manage training sessions

Member Monitoring

  • View member profiles
  • Review attendance history

  • Track engagement levels

Progress Tracking

  • Record observations
  • Monitor training goals
  • Track improvements
  • Review achievements

Communication

  • Review class updates
  • Coordinate training activities
  • Stay informed of schedule changes
