ADMINISTRATION

Overview

GNEX 360 provides administrators with complete control over gym operations through a centralized management system. Administrators are responsible for processing membership applications, managing members and employees, monitoring attendance records, maintaining membership plans, generating reports, and overseeing daily gym activities.

ADMIN PRIVILEGES

Administrator Capabilities

Administrators have access to all management modules within GNEX 360. The following capabilities are available through the administrative dashboard.

Member Management

  • Create member records
  • Update member information
  • Deactivate accounts
  • Monitor memberships

Employee Management

  • Invite employees
  • Assign roles
  • Update permissions
  • Deactivate employee accounts

Membership Plans

  • Create plans
  • Modify pricing
  • Manage subscriptions
  • Track plan usage

Reports & Analytics

  • Attendance reports
  • Revenue reports
  • Membership reports
  • Predictive analytics

QR Administration

  • Validate attendance
  • Monitor QR activity
  • Resolve attendance issues
  • Verify member access

System Settings

  • Configure preferences
  • Manage notifications
  • Control permissions
  • Maintain system policies

MEMBER APPROVALS

Waitlist Management

Membership applications submitted by prospective members are placed in the waitlist module for administrator review. Administrators evaluate each application before approving or rejecting access to the gym.

1

Review Submitted Application

Review the applicant's personal information, selected membership plan, and registration details.

Application Review
2

Verify Payment Information

Confirm whether the submitted payment has been successfully completed and validated.

Payment Verification
3

Approve or Reject Application

Administrators may approve qualified applicants or reject applications that do not meet requirements.

Approval Decision
4

Generate Member Account

Once approved, the system automatically prepares the member account for activation.

Account Creation

Employee Management
