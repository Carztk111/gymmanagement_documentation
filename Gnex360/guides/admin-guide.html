Overview

Administrators oversee daily gym operations through GNEX 360. They manage members, approve applications, monitor attendance, maintain employee records, process memberships, and generate reports.

Administrator Responsibilities

Manage Members

Create, update, activate, and deactivate member accounts.

Approve Applications

Review waitlisted applicants and process approvals.

Monitor Attendance

Track attendance records and gym activity.

Generate Reports

Produce operational and performance reports.
