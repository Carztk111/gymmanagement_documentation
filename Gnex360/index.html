Frequently Asked Questions

01

How do members register?

Members may create an account through the registration page. Submitted applications are reviewed and approved by an administrator before access is granted.

02

Can members change membership plans?

Members may request plan upgrades, renewals, or modifications through the membership management process.

03

How does QR Check-In work?

Members scan their assigned QR code during gym entry. Attendance records are automatically stored by the system.

04

Where can attendance records be viewed?

Attendance history may be accessed through the member dashboard and attendance tracking modules.

05

How are membership requests approved?

Administrators review submitted applications and either approve or reject them based on gym policies.

06

Can reports be generated?

Yes. Administrators may generate attendance, membership, payment, and analytics reports.

07

How are coaches managed?

Coaches are managed through the employee management module where schedules and assignments are configured.

08

Is member information secure?

GNEX 360 uses role-based access control and authentication mechanisms to protect system data.
