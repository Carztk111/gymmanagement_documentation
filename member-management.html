Overview

The Member Management module is the central repository for all member-related information within GNEX 360. It allows gym administrators and staff to manage memberships, review applications, monitor attendance, track transactions, review violations, and control account access throughout the member lifecycle.

Member Directory

The Member Directory provides a searchable and filterable list of all registered members. Administrators can quickly locate member records and access detailed profile information.

Field Description
Member ID Unique system-generated identifier.
Full Name Registered member name.
Email Address Login and communication credential.
Contact Number Primary contact information.
Membership Plan Current subscribed membership package.
Status Active, Expired, Suspended, or Inactive.

Waitlist Management

New applicants enter the waitlist before becoming registered gym members. Administrators can review, approve, or reject submitted applications.

Waitlist Actions

Based on the QA-tested workflows, administrators can perform multiple actions on waitlisted applicants.

View Requestor

Review applicant information including personal details, emergency contact information, and selected membership plan.

Update Requestor

Modify applicant information before approval or account creation.

Approve Requestor

Convert approved applicants into active member accounts.

Reject Requestor

Deny applications and record the reason for rejection.

Member Profiles

Each member profile stores complete personal, membership, attendance, and account information.

Personal Information

Name, birthday, address, gender, and contact details.

Emergency Contact

Emergency contact person, relationship, and phone number.

Membership Details

Active plan, start date, expiration date, and status.

Account History

Attendance logs, transactions, violations, and account activity.

Attendance Tracking

Attendance records are automatically generated through the QR Check-In System and synchronized with reporting modules.

QR Validation

Membership status is validated before attendance is recorded.

Attendance Logs

Stores complete check-in and activity history.

Real-Time Monitoring

View gym occupancy and active member activity.

Transaction Logs

Transaction records track all financial activities associated with member accounts.

Transaction Type Description
Membership Payment Subscription payments.
Membership Renewal Extension of active membership.
Plan Upgrade Upgrade to higher-tier plans.
Refund Request Refund processing records.
Manual Adjustment Administrative modifications.

Violation Logs

Violation records allow administrators to document disciplinary actions and member policy infractions.

Field Description

Violation Type Category of policy breach.
Description Detailed explanation.
Penalty Assigned disciplinary action.
Status Open, Resolved, or Closed.
Logged By Administrator who recorded the violation.
