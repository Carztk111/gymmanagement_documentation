Overview

The Member Management module serves as the central hub for managing gym members throughout their lifecycle. Administrators can approve applications, manage profiles, monitor memberships, review attendance, track transactions, and enforce member policies.

Member Directory

The Member Directory provides a searchable and filterable table containing all registered members.

Field Description
Member Name Primary member identifier.

Phone Number Member contact information.
Address Residential information.
